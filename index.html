About Us

JGR College (جامعة غوثية رضوية) is a prestigious Islamic institute dedicated to teaching the classical Dars-e-Nizami curriculum. Our mission is to preserve and transmit the rich scholarly heritage of the Ummah through structured and authentic learning.

Our Headteacher

Our headteacher is an expert in Arabic language, Fiqh, Hadith, and Tafsir, and a distinguished graduate of Jamia Tul Ashrafiyyah Mubarakpur.

He has studied under renowned scholars such as:

  • Sharih al-Bukhari Mufti Sharif ul Haq Amjadi
  • Siraj al-Fuqaha Mufti Nizamuddin Misbahi
  • Ustadh al-Ulama Allamah Muhammad Ahmed Misbahi
  • Muhaddith al-Kabir Mufti Zia ul Mustafa Amjadi

Our Values

Our methodology aligns with the righteous Salaf and upholds the doctrine of Imam Ahmad Rida Khan. We emphasise authenticity, spiritual refinement, and academic excellence rooted in the classical Sunni tradition.

Our Graduates

Our graduates are actively teaching across the North West and are leading the next generation of scholars, imams, and educators. They continue to spread authentic knowledge and uphold the legacy of traditional scholarship.
