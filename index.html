Dars-e-Nizami • Classical Scholarship • Modern Delivery

JGR COLLEGE
A Centre of Sacred Knowledge
JGR College (جامعة غوثية رضوية) is devoted to preserving the classical Dars-e-Nizami tradition while delivering it through a modern, structured teaching approach.
What sets our programme apart is the balance between traditional depth and modern clarity — enabling students to truly understand the sciences, not just study them.
Our Methodology
We follow the creed and methodology of the Salaf and uphold the doctrine of Imam Ahmad Rida Khan, combining academic precision with spiritual refinement.
Our Headteacher
With over 25 years of teaching experience, our headteacher is a master of Arabic, Fiqh, Hadith, and Tafsir, and holds numerous ijazahs from a multitude of scholars.
