About
A legacy of knowledge
JGR College (جامعة غوثية رضوية) is a distinguished Islamic institute dedicated to preserving and transmitting the classical Dars-e-Nizami tradition. Our goal is to produce scholars grounded in authentic knowledge and spiritual refinement.
Mission
Cultivating scholars
We aim to nurture students into well-rounded scholars who combine deep understanding of Islamic sciences with strong character, discipline, and sincerity.
Headteacher
Scholarly excellence

Curriculum
Classical Dars-e-Nizami
Nahw — Imdad ul Nahw, Nahw Mir, Hidayahtun Nahw
Sarf — Sarf ul Jilani, Imdad ul Sarf
Usul al-Hadith — Mizaan ul Hadith
Tafsir — Tafsir al-Jalalayn
Hadith — Mishkat, Muwatta Imam Muhammad, Sahih Bukhari
Fiqh — Quduri, Wiqayah, Hidayah
Methodology
Rooted in tradition
Our teaching follows the methodology of the Salaf and the doctrine of Imam Ahmad Rida Khan, combining deep academic study with spiritual development and adherence to Sunni tradition.
Student Development
Beyond the classroom
Students are trained not only in knowledge but also in teaching, leadership, and community service, preparing them to guide future generations.
Graduates
Serving the Ummah
Our graduates teach across the North West and beyond, leading institutions and educating communities while preserving the legacy of traditional Islamic scholarship.
