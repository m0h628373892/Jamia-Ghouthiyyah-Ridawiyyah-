About

A Return to Authentic Scholarship

JGR College (جامعة غوثية رضوية) is an Islamic institute dedicated to preserving the classical Dars-e-Nizami tradition through structured, authentic learning rooted in centuries of scholarship.

Headteacher

Scholarly Leadership

Our headteacher is a specialist in Arabic, Fiqh, Hadith, and Tafsir, and a graduate of Jamia Tul Ashrafiyyah Mubarakpur. He has studied under distinguished scholars including:

  • Mufti Sharif ul Haq Amjadi
  • Mufti Nizamuddin Misbahi
  • Allamah Muhammad Ahmed Misbahi
  • Mufti Zia ul Mustafa Amjadi

Curriculum

Classical Dars-e-Nizami

Nahw: Imdad ul Nahw, Nahw Mir, Hidayahtun Nahw

Sarf: Sarf ul Jilani, Imdad ul Sarf

Usul al-Hadith: Mizaan ul Hadith

Tafsir: Tafsir al-Jalalayn

Hadith: Mishkat, Muwatta Imam Muhammad, Sahih Bukhari

Fiqh: Quduri, Wiqayah, Hidayah

Methodology

Rooted in Tradition

Our approach aligns with the Salaf and upholds the doctrine of Imam Ahmad Rida Khan, combining deep spirituality with rigorous academic study.

Graduates

Shaping the Future

Our graduates serve across the North West, teaching and guiding communities, carrying forward the legacy of traditional Islamic scholarship.
